Main Questions faq

What is Tesla Coin?

Tesla Coin is an advanced trading platform that utilizes AI technology to analyze market trends and execute trades with high precision.

1
Who can participate in Tesla Coin Platform?

Any person or corporation from any country can register an account with us, with or without any trading knowledge and we will be glad to accept traders from anywhere in the world.

2
What do I need to join to Tesla Coin?

Starting with Tesla Coin is very simple → just create an account on our platform and complete the verification process. Creating an account takes no longer than 1 minute and gives you access to all the features of Ai Bots.

3
Do I need prior trading experience to use Tesla Coin?

No, Tesla Coin Ais designed for both beginners and experienced traders. We offer educational resources to help you get started.

4
Can I Withdraw My Deposit Anytime?

No, your deposit (principal) is locked during the duration of the ai trades.

5
How do I activate an Ai Bot?

To activate a bot, you need to decide which bot you prefer, then make a deposit in accordance to the bots portfolio range. Once the deposit is approved by the system you can go ahead to active your bot by clicking on bots section in your dashboard.

6
I already have an activated bot, I want more, what should I do?

You can activate an additional bot at any time, select your preferred bot, make a new deposit into your dashboard and then click on activate.

7
Can I Register More Than One Account?

No, having more than one account per person is not allowed.

8
How Can I Change My Password?

You can change your password in your account's profile settings.

9
How Do I Make a Deposit?

Login to your Tesla Coin account and click Deposit in the menu. Choose the payment method and follow the instructions to complete the transaction.

10
What Is the Minimum and Maximum Amount I Can Deposit?

You can invest any amount between $500 to Infinity.

11
How to add my payment details?

Payment details are added during withdrawals, in other for us to help you track the particular wallet address you want to receive the payment per withdrawals. Log in to your dashboard, go to the Withdral section, Choose Wallet and fill the form of your wallet address.

12
How Long Does It Take For My Deposit to Appear In My Account?

Your deposit will be credited to your account immediately upon receiving the funds. For cryptocurrency transactions, up to 6 blockchain confirmations may be required before finishing the transaction.

13
Can I Direct Deposit Funds From My Account Balance?

Yes, you can use your balance to make a new deposit without withdrawing the funds first.

How Do I Make a Withdrawal?

You can withdraw your available account balance anytime. Login to your Tesla Coin account and click Withdraw in the menu. Follow the instructions to complete the withdrawal process.

How long do you process the withdrawal of funds?

Withdrawal of funds is processing up to 24 hours since submitting a request.

You can use your affiliate link to invite new investors to Tesla Coin. You will receive a commission whenever the new investor makes a deposit.
